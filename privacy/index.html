Privacy Policy — White Noise App

Developer: CosmoApps • Effective date: 2025-10-17

1. Introduction

White Noise (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using the White Noise mobile application (“App”), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to ensure stable app operation and improve user experience.

2.1. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device model, OS version, language, region, and app version;
  • Usage statistics (e.g., which sounds are played, duration of sessions);
  • App performance and crash data;
  • Advertising and analytics identifiers (IDFA, GAID, etc.);
  • Country and time zone (approximate, non-precise).

All this data is anonymized and not linked to personally identifiable information.

2.2. Subscription and Payment Data

  • Payments are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store.
  • We do not receive or store your payment card or Apple ID details.
  • We may receive anonymous transaction data (e.g., successful subscription or renewal) to unlock premium features.

2.3. No Sensitive Data

We do not collect user-generated content, photos, contacts, microphone, camera, or precise location data.

3. How We Use the Data

  • Ensure app stability and fix technical issues;
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve user experience;
  • Manage premium subscriptions and access control;
  • Measure marketing campaign effectiveness and user retention.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is processed using secure, industry-standard methods (SSL/TLS, encryption in transit and at rest).
  • We do not host personal data on our own servers — all data is processed by verified third-party providers listed below.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services for analytics, monetization, and marketing attribution:

  • Amplitude — analyze user behavior and feature performance. Amplitude Privacy Policy
  • Adapty — manage in-app purchases, subscriptions, and paywall analytics. Adapty Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google LLC) — performance monitoring, crash reporting, aggregated analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • AppsFlyer — attribution, campaign measurement, user acquisition analytics. AppsFlyer Privacy Policy

Each service processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy and may store it on servers located worldwide, including in the United States or the European Union.

6. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), data processing is based on:

  • Legitimate interest (app operation and analytics);
  • Contract necessity (subscription management);
  • User consent (for advertising identifiers and tracking, where applicable).

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. Anonymous analytics data may be stored longer for aggregated statistical analysis.

8. Your Rights

You may have rights under applicable law (e.g., access, deletion, portability, objection). To exercise your rights, contact us at cosmoapps13@gmail.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors. If you believe your child provided data to us, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the App and/or on our website, with the updated “Effective date”.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data processing, please contact us at: cosmoapps13@gmail.com
